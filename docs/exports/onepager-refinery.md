# Data Refinery — datasets with receipts

**Retrieval-ready datasets where every chunk knows where it came from.**

The Refinery harvests, chunks, and catalogs text corpora with full
provenance: source, license posture, consent receipt, and an OKF
dataset card per entry. A self-writing wiki documents every dataset and
rebuilds automatically after each harvest — the catalog can't drift
from its documentation.

**Live now (data.bhenre.com):** browsable catalog with per-dataset
provenance and free samples; custom harvest requests; consented
contribution pipeline with erasure honored by receipt.

**Consent, structurally:** submissions without the consent box checked
are analyzed and discarded — storage requires the explicit opt-in, and
every stored contribution carries a receipt that can be quoted for
erasure.

**For customers of the recommendation platform:** your uploaded corpora
get the same treatment — provenance, cards, and a wiki page — so your
data assets are documented as a side effect of using them.

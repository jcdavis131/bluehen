# Simulation Lab — strategy research, simulation-only

**Paper-simulated market strategy reports — the generality stress test
for the Blue Hen platform.**

The Simulation Lab runs the same learning loop that powers our
retrieval products against market-prediction fixtures: skills are
benchmarked, gated on non-regression against baselines, and reported
with their full methodology.

**The wall, stated plainly:** everything here is simulation-only. No
live capital, no order routing, no trading advice — every page, report,
and result is labeled as fixture/paper simulation. That's not a
disclaimer bolted on; it's the product's design boundary (Spec 0013).

**Why it exists:** a platform that claims domain generality has to
prove it outside its home domain. Market microstructure text and
event streams are dense, adversarial, and nothing like research
abstracts — if the loop's gates hold here, the "any domain" claim has
teeth.

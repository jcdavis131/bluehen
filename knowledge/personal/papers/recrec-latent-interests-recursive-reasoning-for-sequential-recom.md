Sequential recommender systems rely on a single forward pass to encode user interaction histories and predict the next item. Increasing inference-time computation through latent reasoning, with the model proceeding step by step before the final prediction, has been recently explored in sequential recommendation with promising results. However, how to structure the reasoning process for sequential recommendation remains an open question.

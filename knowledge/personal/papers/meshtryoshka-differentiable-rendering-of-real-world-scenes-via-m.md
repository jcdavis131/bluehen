Differentiable rendering has emerged as a powerful approach for 3D reconstruction and novel view synthesis. State-of-the-art differentiable rendering methods combine a variety of custom representations of 3D geometry and appearance with specialized renderers. However, most downstream tasks in computer graphics rely on 3D meshes.
